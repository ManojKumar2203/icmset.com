About The Conference

Overview

Modern engineering practices are rapidly evolving toward sustainability due to growing global environmental challenges and technological advancements. ICMSET 2K26 aims to bring together researchers, academicians, industry experts, and technologists across disciplines to discuss innovations that support sustainable development.

The conference provides a platform for presenting cutting-edge research, exchanging scientific knowledge, and fostering collaborative research in emerging areas of engineering, sustainability, and technology.

Key Tracks

  • Sustainable materials, green manufacturing and advanced design concepts
  • Renewable energy systems, energy storage and environmental technologies
  • Automotive, aerospace, mobility engineering and intelligent systems
  • Robotics, AI, digital engineering and Industry 4.0 applications

ABOUT US

Panimalar Engineering College, a Christian Minority Institution under JaiSakthi Educational Trust, is affiliated with Anna University, Chennai and approved by UGC for 12(B) and 2(f) status for research activities. Situated on a serene 52.87-acre campus at Poonamallee, Chennai, the college is known for academic excellence, discipline, cutting-edge infrastructure, and strong value-based education.

The institution offers seven UG and three PG programmes in Engineering and Management. Accredited by NBA, the college fosters a rich learning atmosphere supported by experienced, research-oriented faculty members and advanced laboratories. The campus maintains strong associations with professional bodies like IEEE, ISTE, IETE, CSI, SAE INDIA and many more.
