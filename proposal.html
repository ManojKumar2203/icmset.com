PROPOSAL

Proposals are invited for keynote speakers/ Session speakers from academicians and industry experts from highly-reputed organizations. The proposals should clearly indicate:

  1. Title (domain)
  2. Name(s), designation, and affiliation
  3. Aims and Objective

Session speakers proposal along with a short curriculum vitae can be mailed to ICMSET@panimalar.ac.in / ICMSET2k26@gmail.com on or before 5th March 2026. After the decision of ICMSET session members regarding the selection, confirmation will be intimated within 15 days from the last date of submission.

NOTES:

  • All approved speakers will be listed under the SPECIAL SESSIONS tab on the ICMSET website.
  • The speakers will be offered 2AC train travel in India and accommodation during the session.
  • Each special session will be appreciated with a momento.
