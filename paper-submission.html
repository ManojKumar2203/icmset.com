REVIEW PROCESS

  1. The submissions are generally reviewed for relevance with the conference scope, novelty against current state of the art, significance of the research contribution and English language.
  2. All submitted papers will be checked for plagiarism report using Turnitin. If the paper contains more than 15% plagiarism, review process will not be carried.
  3. ICMSET research papers are reviewed using a single-blind review process managed through CMT. Each paper is reviewed independently by two reviewers, and assigned reviewers submit their anonymous reviews through CMT.
  4. ICMSET TPC then select the papers for conference proceeding based on reviewers score and comments.
